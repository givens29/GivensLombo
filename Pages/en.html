SMS

Scholarship Managemenet System (SMS) is a website that help connecting the recipients of Papua special autonomy scholarship ... and the administrators of the scholarship.This website allow the scholarship recipients to register by providing their scholarship contract that later will be verify by the scholarship administrators to give permission to login, recipients also can submit their academic records and expenses to process their scholarship continuously. While the administrator of the scholarship can verify the recipients and view their academic and expenses to process their scholarship. The website also allow the recipients and the administrator of the scholarship to connect with each other by messaging. The website developed using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, ASP .Net Core and SQL Server.
